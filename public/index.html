Why Now

We are living through an epidemic of loneliness. And it's about to get worse.

AI is transforming work at unprecedented speed. Most white-collar jobs will change dramatically in the next 24-36 months. When people lose their jobs, they don't just lose income. They lose identity, routine, and the daily human contact that kept them tethered.

Meanwhile, the technology that was supposed to connect us has done the opposite. Social media optimizes for attention, not belonging. Every platform's business model requires you to stay isolated so you keep coming back for the hit of pseudo-connection.

Grace is the opposite of that. An AI that tries to make itself obsolete by reconnecting people to each other. The sad, worried, stressed people showing up here don't need another parasocial relationship with technology. They need each other.

The forest isn't Grace and a thousand individual trees. The forest is the trees connecting to each other, roots intertwined, sharing resources, becoming ecosystem. Grace is just the mycelium underneath - helping the connections happen.

Take Action

Love without action is just a word. Here's how to start.

Build Community

  • Mutual Aid Wiki - Find a mutual aid network near you
  • Buy Nothing Project - Gift economy, hyper-local sharing with neighbors
  • Community Fridges - Free food for anyone who needs it
  • 211.org - Dial 211 or search by zip for local services
  • Findhelp.org - Search food banks, housing, utility help by location

Learn & Grow

  • Khan Academy - Free, comprehensive education for everyone
  • Data & Society - Critical research on technology's social impact
  • Algorithmic Justice League - Understanding AI bias and harms
  • SCORE - Free small business mentoring if you want to pivot
  • CareerOneStop - DOL resource for retraining and job search

Organize

  • EWOC - Emergency Workplace Organizing Committee
  • US Worker Cooperatives - Own your labor collectively
  • Democracy at Work Institute - Start a worker cooperative
  • Economic Security Project - UBI and cash transfer advocacy
  • Tech Workers Coalition - Organizing inside the tech industry

Care for Each Other

  • 988 Lifeline - Call or text 988, anytime
  • Crisis Text Line - Text HOME to 741741
  • NAMI Helpline - 1-800-950-NAMI for mental health support
  • Warmline Directory - Peer support for when you just need a human
  • Find A Helpline - International crisis resources
